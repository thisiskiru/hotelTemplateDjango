Welcome To YegofeView Hotel.

Yegofe View Hotel is a well-established tree-star hotel found at a convenient location in the center of the city Kombolcha. The name derives from “Yegofe Mountain (የጎፍ ተራራ)”, the biggest and known mountain in Kombolcha. A number of features make us superb and different from others including our services we provide to the best satisfaction of our guests. The business amenities we offer including a 24-hour business center and a meeting hall that accommodates up-to 500 persons at a time creates a convenient atmosphere for business travelers to hold meetings and conclude agreements with their counterparts. Complimentary wireless internet access is provided in public areas, lobby and rooms. Self-parking is complimentary and we also provide a round trip airport shuttle on request.
